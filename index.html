Who are we?

We are KAIZER Farms

A 50 sow farrow-to-finish open-pen operation, which utilizes IMO and currently produces up to 600 pigs annually. We produce pigs that are sold to various food vendors, restaurants and to customers at the various local markets. Here they are harvested and processed for major grocery food chains. We currently have three employees working in the hog operation. Ato Wright has managed the pork operation for the past 2 years. Our consulting herd veterinarian is Dr. Peter Schneider, DVM.
